Common questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Open Zenith, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in to the App Store with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

How do I manage or cancel Zenith Pro?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone, open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Zenith, where you can change plan or cancel. Cancel at least 24 hours before your renewal date to avoid the next charge.

What's included in the free version?

You can track up to 3 habits, use the full to-do list, Home Screen widgets, and Live Activities for free. Zenith Pro unlocks unlimited habits, the full forest, voice Brain Dump and AI features, Focus mode, deep insights, custom app icons, and iCloud sync.

How is my data handled?

Your habits and tasks live on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details, and our Terms of Use.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual items in the app, or remove everything by deleting the app. If you created an optional account, you can delete it from Settings inside the app.
