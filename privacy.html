Privacy Policy

Zenith Habit Tracker · Last updated: 6 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Zenith ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. Zenith is built to be private by default: your habit and task data lives on your device, and we do not track you or sell your data.

Who we are

Zenith is developed by Kyle Keuris, based in Melbourne, Australia. You can reach us at zenithhabittracker@gmail.com.

The short version

  • We do not track you, show ads, or use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • Your habits, tasks, notes, streaks and check-ins are stored locally on your device.
  • The only data that leaves your device is when you use voice/AI features or subscribe — described below.
  • You can delete all of your data at any time by deleting the app.

Information stored on your device

Zenith stores the content you create — habits, tasks, sub-tasks, completion history, streaks, mood and energy check-ins, focus sessions, and your settings — locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. This data is not transmitted to us. If you have iCloud device backup enabled, this data may be included in your encrypted Apple backup under Apple's terms.

Voice "Brain Dump" and AI features

When you use voice input, your speech is transcribed using Apple's on-device / Apple-operated Speech Recognition, which is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. The resulting text (or text you type) is sent over an encrypted connection to our AI processing service, operated on our behalf by Groq, solely to turn your input into structured tasks and insights. This text is processed to generate your result and is not used to advertise to you or to identify you personally. We do not attach your name or contact details to these requests; a random per-install identifier is used only to enforce fair-use limits.

Subscriptions and payments

Zenith Pro is sold as an auto-renewing subscription through the Apple App Store. Apple processes your payment and manages your subscription — we never receive or store your payment card details. We record anonymous, on-device events about subscription actions (e.g. trial started, purchase completed) to understand the app's performance; these stay on your device unless you choose to share diagnostics with us.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device. We do not operate a push server and do not send you marketing notifications.

Health & fitness information

Habit and wellness data you enter (for example a meditation or exercise habit, or mood/energy check-ins) may be considered health & fitness information. This data stays on your device and is used only to provide the app's features. Zenith does not read from or write to Apple Health.

Accounts

Zenith does not currently require an account to use, and we do not operate a user account server. If account sign-in is offered, any credentials entered are handled on-device and this policy will be updated before any account data is transmitted off your device.

Children

Zenith is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country) and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Your rights

Because your content is stored on your device, you control it directly: edit or delete items in the app, or remove everything by deleting the app. For any questions about your data, contact us at zenithhabittracker@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date.
